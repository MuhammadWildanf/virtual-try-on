Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ImajiwaLab ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Virtual Try-On application located at tryon.imajiwa.id (the "Service").

1. Camera Access and Usage

Our Service utilizes Augmented Reality (AR) technology powered by Snap Camera Kit to provide a virtual clothing try-on experience. To function, the Service requires access to your device's camera.

Important: We do not record, store, or transmit your video feed or facial data to our servers. The camera feed is processed entirely locally on your device in real-time to overlay the 3D augmented reality clothing items. Once you close the application or browser tab, the camera access terminates immediately.

2. Data Collection

Because the AR processing happens locally on your device, we do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or biometric data through the camera feed.

We only collect anonymous usage data (such as page views or button clicks) to improve the performance of our website.

3. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates the Snap Camera Kit SDK provided by Snap Inc. The SDK processes your camera feed locally to render the Augmented Reality lenses. Snap Inc. may collect anonymous, aggregated usage and performance metrics related to the SDK's operation. For more information on how Snap Inc. handles data, please refer to the Snap Inc. Privacy Policy.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our use of your camera data, please contact us at our official channels.
